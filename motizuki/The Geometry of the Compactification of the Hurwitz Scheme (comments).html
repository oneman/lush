COMMENTS ON “THE GEOMETRY OF THE COMPACTIFICATION OF THE HURWITZ SCHEME” Shinichi Mochizuki August 2017 (1.) In the statement of the Theorem of §3.22, the deﬁnition of the morphisms of the stack under consideration is only given explicitly in the case where the domain and codomain of the morphism are identical; in fact, however, the deﬁnition should [and, indeed, can easily] be given for morphisms for which the domain and codomain of the morphism are not necessarily identical. (2.) In §4.6, (2), (B), β), the phrase “ramiﬁcation)going” should read “ramiﬁcation) going”. (3.) In the illustration given in the Pictorial Appendix for Species 3B ∗ , the low- ermost irreducible component on the right-hand side of the domain curve of the covering [i.e., the irreducible component marked by the phrase “one copy of P 1 ”] should be deleted.
