COMMENTS ON “A PANORAMIC OVERVIEW OF INTER-UNIVERSAL TEICHMÜLLER THEORY” Shinichi Mochizuki November 2015 (1.) In the discussion towards the end of §3, the word “rigidity” is misspelled (i.e., “ridigity”: 3 instances). (2.) In the ﬁnal sentence of §1, the phrase “content of the so-called Szpiro Con- jecture” should be replaced by the phrase “content of a certain strong version of the so-called Szpiro Conjecture”. The term “Szpiro Conjecture” as it appears in the Abstract and Corollary 4.2 should also be understood as referring to this strong version. (3.) Throughout the manuscript, the phrases “topological monoid(s)” and “topo- logical module” should be replaced, respectively, by the phrases “ind-topological monoid(s)” and “ind-topological module”.
